Janan Gold

Janan Gold is the epitome of sophistication, designed for those who seek elegance and grace. This exquisite fragrance captures the warmth of golden sunlight and the richness of rare ingredients. Top notes of citrus and honeyed florals open the scent with a radiant burst, while a heart of luxurious oud and amber weaves a story of timeless allure. Perfect for evenings of refinement or moments when you want to leave a lasting impression.

Janan Gold

  1. Inspiration: The fragrance concept is inspired by the golden hour's warmth and elegance, aiming to evoke a sense of luxury and timeless beauty.
  2. Ingredient Selection: Premium ingredients such as citrus, honeyed florals, oud, and amber are carefully sourced to create a unique and captivating aroma.
  3. Fragrance Formulation: Expert perfumers craft the perfect balance of top, heart, and base notes to ensure the scent unfolds beautifully over time.
  4. Bottle and Packaging Design: A sophisticated gold-themed bottle is designed to reflect the luxurious essence of the perfume, complemented by elegant packaging.
  5. Quality Assurance: The fragrance undergoes rigorous testing to ensure the highest standards in scent longevity, projection, and overall quality.
  6. Launch and Delivery: Janan Gold is introduced to the market through a well-planned launch, ensuring it reaches customers who seek luxury and refinement.
